Carbon Footprint Food Calculator

Your carbon footprint calculated above takes into account the emissions involved in processes like farming, processing, packaging, and transporting food. It is a measure of

how much CO2 is released

into the atmosphere as a result

Carbon Dioxide is one of the main culprits of global warming. So the more CO2 emitted, the greater the consequences of climate change (rising sea levels, extreme weather eek!)
But don't worry, there are loads of ways you can help!

Reduce food waste

- One third of all food produced is wasted,
representing 14 million tonnes of carbon dioxide emissions in the UK (equivalent to the CO2 produced by 7 million cars each year!)
